THE HISTORY OF THE MESEUM

The history of the meseum

Research Institute for Languages and Culture of Asia (RILCA) was firstly established in a name of "Research Center for Languages and Cultures of Southeast Asia" in 1974 to produce Linguistic academicians that are advantage in communicating with people on diversity race, languages and cultures In 1981, was changed to "Research Center for Languages and Cultures for Rural Development" and lastly became ""Research Center for Languages and Cultures of Asia" in 2009.
